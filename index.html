About Us

YEEM is a youth-led organisation empowering communities to address climate change, biodiversity loss, and pollution through innovation, climate action, digital skills, and inclusive, community-driven solutions.

Our Mission

To empower youth and communities to address environmental challenges through sustainable solutions that enhance climate resilience, digital literacy, and policy advocacy.

Our Vision

A sustainable future where young people lead in solving environmental challenges through innovation, collaboration, and inclusive action.

Our Objectives

  • Youth empowerment and leadership development
  • Climate resilience and adaptation
  • Digital innovation for sustainability
  • Biodiversity conservation and ecosystem restoration
  • Inclusive community development
  • Youth-led policy advocacy
  • Global collaboration and partnerships

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Long-term environmental and social impact
  • Inclusivity: Ensuring all voices are heard
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas and technologies
  • Collaboration: Working with partners and communities
  • Empowerment: Enabling youth to lead change
